Dexter

On my last assignment, I said that Breaking Bad was my favorite show. I would say it still is, but I've recently started watching Dexter and I'm really enjoying it. I think the main reason I like the show so much is due to how the main character is portrayed. He is a serial killer that is living a normal life and only "takes care" of bad people. I like psychology so it's fun to see how the writers of the show gave a serial killer a moral compass and construct what his daily life involves.
