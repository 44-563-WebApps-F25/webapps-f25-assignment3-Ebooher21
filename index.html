How to Brush Your Teeth

  1. Preperations
    • Toothbrush
    • Toothpaste
    • Floss
    • Mouthwash
  2. Floss
    • In between each of your teeth, you'll want to gently run the floss back and forth near the gums.
  3. Optional: Warm up toothbrush
    • I usually run hot water over my toothbrush to soften the bristles.
  4. Put toothpast on the brush
    • You don't need a lot.
  5. Gently Scrub your teeth in a circular back and forth motion
    • You'll want to do this for about 2 minutes.
    • Don't forget your tongue so your breath doesn't smell bad.
  6. Spit, rinse, and use some mouthwash
    • You'll want to swish the wash back and forth for about 30 seconds.
    • Then gurgle for a little bit to get the back of your mouth and spit.
  7. Boom, now you have clean teeth
